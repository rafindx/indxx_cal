Corporate Actions Administration

(CAdmin)

OPTIMIZE AUTOMATION & MINIMISE RISK

OVERVIEW

Our Corporate Actions Administration Platform (CAdmin) is a centralized, integrated hub of all ordinary and extraordinary corporate actions that can be accessed via a flexible delivery mechanism. CAdmin helps minimize the errors that exist across data from multiple sources.

The golden copy is maintained via an automated engine that compiles and integrates corporate action feeds from multiple sources, automatically merges the data, and identifies and reconciles the difference in case of mismatch.

WHY US?

Our offerings have been built to address widespread challenges faced by users in processing the corporate action data from multiple sources and/or vendors. We recognize the value of the timely notification of a corporate action and how a missed corporate action can create extensive operational risks.

With our wide range of capabilities, 15 years of legacy and a strong 200+ member team based in the United States and India, our corporate action offering helps add cost efficiencies across the asset management value chain.

OUR FEATURES

Our Corporate Actions Administration Platform (CAdmin) is designed to provide best-in-class features that enable our clients to make smarter decisions through validated corporate actions.
